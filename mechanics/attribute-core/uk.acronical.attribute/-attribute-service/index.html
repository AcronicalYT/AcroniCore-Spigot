AttributeService

open class AttributeService

A service for managing entity attributes and modifiers using NamespacedKeys.

This service simplifies the application and removal of modifiers, ensuring that plugin-specific changes are tracked and can be cleared without affecting vanilla or other plugins' modifiers.
